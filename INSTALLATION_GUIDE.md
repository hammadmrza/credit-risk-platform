## Installation — two options

### Option A: Fresh install (recommended if you haven't modified anything since my last package)

1. **Back up your current project folder** (just in case):
   - Right-click `C:\Users\hamma\Desktop\Credit-Risk-Platform` → Copy → Paste → rename to
     `Credit-Risk-Platform-backup-2026-04-21`.

2. **Delete the current folder contents** (NOT the backup):
   - Open `C:\Users\hamma\Desktop\Credit-Risk-Platform`
   - Delete everything inside.

3. **Extract this zip** into `C:\Users\hamma\Desktop\Credit-Risk-Platform\`:
   - The zip root is `Credit-Risk-Platform/`, so after extraction your folder should look like
     the tree above.

4. **Copy your `data/raw/` CSV files back in** from the backup:
   - `lending_club_loans.csv`
   - `heloc_dataset_v1.csv`
   - Place them in `Credit-Risk-Platform/data/raw/`.

5. **Copy your trained `models/*.pkl` back in** from the backup (optional — saves a rebuild):
   - All 12 `.pkl` files from `models/` in your backup.
   - If you skip this, run `python build.py` to regenerate them.

6. **Copy your `data/processed/` parquet files back in** from the backup (optional):
   - Saves you 15-25 minutes of rebuild time.

## Launching the app

```bash
cd C:\Users\hamma\Desktop\Credit-Risk-Platform
streamlit run src/app/streamlit_app.py
```

Opens in your browser at `http://localhost:8501`. All 7 tabs should render and the
Executive Dashboard (Tab 7) will show the corrected CAR of 11.50% (the assumption),
not the bogus 26.87% you'd see under the v1.0 bug.

## Training the new v1.1 segmented models (optional)

If you want real per-product AUC numbers instead of the illustrative placeholders:

```bash
python notebooks/phase4b/04b_segmented_models.py
```

This will:
1. Load your processed train + OOT test parquet files (from Phase 1 + 3)
2. Train one XGBoost per product (Unsecured + Secured)
3. Write `models/xgb_pd_unsecured.pkl` and `models/xgb_pd_secured.pkl`
4. Overwrite `reports/phase4/model_comparison_segmented.csv` with real metrics

The Streamlit app picks up the new CSV automatically on next restart.

Takes 5-10 minutes depending on your hardware.

## Verifying everything works

After install, open the app and check:

1. **Tab 1 — Application Assessment**
   - Loan term dropdown shows only [36, 60] when Unsecured is selected (not 12/24/48/120)
   - Loan term dropdown shows only [36] when HELOC is selected
   - Score a clean prime borrower — the fraud strip should be collapsed in an expander

2. **Tab 2 — Batch Scoring**
   - Click "Load Test Portfolio" — 4 filters appear above the results (Product, Risk Tier, Decision, Min EL)
   - Product × Tier concentration table renders below the tier distribution chart

3. **Tab 3 — Model Performance**
   - ROC curve and KS separation curve render side by side
   - Section 3 shows v1.0 vs v1.1 segmented comparison with lift narrative
   - Confusion matrix at PD=28% renders with precision/recall/F1
   - Lift and gains charts render below the confusion matrix

4. **Tab 4 — Compliance**
   - CAR displays as 11.50% (the assumption), not 26.87% or similar
   - IFRS 9 stage rows are colour-coded green/amber/red
   - Stress test explains HELOC LGD stress limitation

5. **Tab 5 — Pricing**
   - Market rate comparison bar chart renders with 6 Canadian lender benchmarks
   - Profit curve renders with annotated profit-max threshold
   - CoF sensitivity chart renders

6. **Tab 6 — Fraud**
   - Fraud trend over time chart renders
   - Product × fraud type drill-down renders

7. **Tab 7 — Executive Dashboard**
   - 7-column KPI row (7th is "CAR (assumed)" at 11.50%)
   - PD distribution shows TWO vertical lines: "Approval threshold (28%)" and "Refer band ceiling (35%)"
   - EL concentration chart clearly distinguishes "% of Loans" (blue) from "% of EL" (red)
   - Empty-portfolio warning appears if you haven't yet built the pipeline

If any of these fail, let me know which and I'll patch.
