† loan_term_months acts as a product-type proxy in the dual-product
  harmonization (36/60-month unsecured vs. imputed-36 HELOC). Its
  high IV reflects the different baseline default rates between the
  two product books, not a genuine relationship between loan term
  and HELOC default risk. The feature is neutralized at scoring time
  by snapping HELOC records to the training imputation value (36).
  See src/app/utils.py:443-448 and MODEL_CARD.md § Known Limitations.
  Separate product models (v1.1) will remove this proxy entirely.
